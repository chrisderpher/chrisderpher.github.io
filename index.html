Intro

I'm a network engineer with a background in windows-environment system admin-ing. I've also got a little bit of a background working in a bakery. Let me know if you need someone to pipe some frosting for a cake! I'm huge into electronics and I spend a lot of time outside of work on projects related to that interest. I've built a few custom 3d printers, one of which is 80% 3d-printed, even the hot-end! I like building racing drones as well - please invite me to race!

I love my soldering iron and I love tinkering with things using it! One of my most recent personal electronics projects was creating a mechanical keyboard 100% from scratch (even the keycaps!). I 3d-printed the body of the keyboard, soldered the matrix of keys to the microcontroller and wrote a firmware to make it all work! By the way, check out my resume if you're interested in learning some more about me!
